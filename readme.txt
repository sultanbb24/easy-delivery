== Installation ==

1. Upload the easy-delivery folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to **Easy Delivery Option** to configure your delivery/Pickup settings.
4. The delivery date field will automatically appear on your Checkout page!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this work with any theme? =
Yes! Easy Delivery is designed to inherit your theme's styles and works perfectly with popular themes like Storefront, Astra, and Divi.

= Where does the delivery date appear for the admin? =
You can see it inside the WooCommerce Orders Details in item section .

= Can I prevent same-day delivery? =
Yes. Simply Checked Disable Same Day from Easy delivery General Setting.
